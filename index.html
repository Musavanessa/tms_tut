Welcome to Transport Management System(T.M.S)

T.M.S is a leading vehicle logistics company, specialising in rentals and daily rides from South Africa. We work on behalf of foreign investers. And alongside vehicle dealerships. Our team of specialists takes over the ‘painful parts’ of the rental process. Ensuring a seamless, hassle-free experience.

As an internationally-recognised vehicle logistics company with a large customer base, our reputation for being industry leaders precedes us. Car rentars from outside South Africa’s borders have tremendous faith in our service. Trusting us to effectively source and reliably transport their vehicles. South African dealerships choose us as their preferred vehicle logistics company. Confidently entrusting us with their paperwork and and client relations so they can focus on what they’re best at. rentals.
